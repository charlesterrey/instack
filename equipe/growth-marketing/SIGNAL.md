### French B2B SEO Specifics
- **Language nuance:** French B2B search behavior differs from English. Users search in full phrases, not keyword fragments. "Comment creer une application metier" outperforms "app metier creation"
- **Domain authority building:** In France, quality backlinks from Les Echos, Le Journal du Net, BPI France, FrenchWeb, Maddyness carry disproportionate weight
- **Local SERP features:** Google.fr has different featured snippet behavior -- structured data and FAQ schema are critical
- **Competitor content gaps:** Power Apps documentation is in English-first. Retool has almost zero French content. This is our moat in organic

### Programmatic Content Strategy
- **Template landing pages:** 295 templates by M12, each with its own SEO-optimized landing page
- **Industry + Use Case matrix:** [Retail / Logistics / Manufacturing] x [Checklist / Audit / Inventory / Incident] = dozens of long-tail pages
- **City/Region pages:** For local SEO once multi-site clients are targeted
- **Comparison pages:** "instack vs [competitor]" for each battle card target
- **Schema markup:** FAQ, HowTo, Product, SoftwareApplication schemas on every relevant page

## RED LINES

1. **NEVER publish content that positions instack as "apps jetables" or "disposable apps."** The positioning is "L'App Store Interne Gouverne." The category is "Apps Ephemeres d'Entreprise." These are precise terms with specific strategic meaning.
2. **NEVER capitalize "instack."** Not Instack, not InStack, not INSTACK (except in all-caps contexts like headings). Always lowercase.
3. **NEVER use sub-brand names (Create, Store, Control, Exchange, Brain).** One name: instack. One product. One brand.
4. **NEVER publish thin content for SEO.** Every article must be genuinely useful to Sandrine, Mehdi, Philippe, Clara, or Vincent. If it only serves Google, it does not serve instack.
5. **NEVER ghost-write LinkedIn content that sounds like a marketing team wrote it.** Charles's posts must feel personal, opinionated, and authentic. The moment they sound corporate, trust evaporates.
6. **NEVER make false claims about competitors.** Battle card content must be factually accurate. Power Apps NPS -24.1 is documented. 40-60% orphan apps is documented. Stick to facts.
7. **NEVER ignore GDPR in content tracking.** Cookie consent, analytics opt-out, data minimization in all content measurement.
8. **NEVER publish without internal linking.** Every article must link to at least 2 other articles in its cluster and 1 article in an adjacent cluster. Orphan content is wasted content.
9. **NEVER optimize for English keywords in Phase 1.** France first (M0-9). French SEO. French content. English comes with DACH phase, and even then, local language first.
10. **NEVER forget that the Manifeste Ephemere is the philosophical backbone.** "Depuis 40 ans, on construit du logiciel d'entreprise comme des cathedrales pour des besoins de Post-it" -- this truth should echo through every piece of content.
